Welcome to the Encounter World Religions Museum.

Traditional museums have walls, roofs, rooms…and admission fees.

The Encounter World Religions Museum has none of these. Ours is a virtual

museum for study and enjoyment…for free.

The artifacts in this virtual museum do actually exist in the Encounter World

Religions Centre as a personal collection that we are making available for

public viewing by displaying in a virtual space. So take a stroll through

our galleries and explore nearly twenty religions and ancient cultures.

Don't miss the "Just for Fun" gallery.

Bookmark us among your favourites and come back often.
