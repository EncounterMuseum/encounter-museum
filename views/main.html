Welcome to the Encounter World Religions Museum.

Traditional museums have walls, roofs, rooms…and admission fees. The Encounter World Religions

Museum has none of these. Ours is a virtual museum for study and enjoyment…for free.

The artifacts in this virtual museum do actually exist in the Encounter World Religions Centre as

a personal collection accumulated over nearly fifty years of study and instruction of diverse

People, Places, Practices and Philosophies from around the world.

Encounter is pleased to make this private acquisition available for public viewing by displaying

this vast collection in a virtual space. So take a virtual stroll through our galleries and explore

nearly twenty religions and ancient cultures…and don’t miss the “Just For Fun” gallery.

Remember to bookmark us among your favourites and come back often.

Look…learn…enjoy.
